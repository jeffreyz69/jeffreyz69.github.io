I believe learning how to build a computer is very important, here's why.

In modern society most tasks are being done digitally, thus it is more important than ever to invest in a computer that will not only get the job done quicker, but also for longer with quality selection of hardware.

Thanks to the modular nature of desktop computers, once you learn how to build it, maintenance becomes trivial (with a bit of research) and its modular feature can allow you to save big in the long run. I'll go more into detail in the details page.
